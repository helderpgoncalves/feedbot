Connecting Telegram is a two-step process:

## 1. (Self-host only) Configure the bot once

In the dashboard's **Settings → Telegram bot**, paste your token from
[@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather) and the bot username. Hit Test
to verify the token works. Save.

The cloud version is pre-configured.

## 2. Connect a chat to a project

Open the project page and click **+ Connect chat**. We generate a
single-use deep link of the form
`https://t.me/<bot>?startgroup=link_<token>`.

Click the link, pick a Telegram group, the bot joins, the chat
appears in your **Chats** tab, and every message starts being
classified.

<Aside type="caution" title="Privacy">
The bot is **not** present in any chat by default. We only see
messages in groups where you explicitly added the bot via a deep
link. To stop, remove the bot from the group or click "Disconnect"
in the dashboard.
</Aside>
