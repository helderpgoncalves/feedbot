If your organisation requires a signed Data Processing Addendum to use
**app.feedbot.dev**, request a copy via
**[email protected]**. We'll counter-sign within 5 business days.

The DPA covers:

- The role split: **you are the controller**; we are the **processor**.
- The categories of personal data processed (mirrors the
  [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy/) §2 table).
- Sub-processor list (Privacy Policy §5) — we'll notify owners by
  email at least 14 days before adding a new sub-processor.
- Data return + deletion procedures: triggered by `Account & data →
  Delete workspace` in the dashboard, with the retention windows in
  Privacy Policy §3.
- Standard contractual clauses for any data transfer outside the EEA
  (today, only Stripe falls into this category for the billing data).

Before signing, please also review the
[Terms of Service](/legal/terms/) and
[Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy/) — the DPA references both.

## When you don't need a DPA

If you're self-hosting Feedbot, no DPA is needed: there's no
controller-processor relationship between you and us. The
[MIT license](https://github.com/helderpgoncalves/feedbot/blob/main/LICENSE)
governs your use of the source code.
