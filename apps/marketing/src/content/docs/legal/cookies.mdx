We use **two cookies on app.feedbot.dev**, both strictly necessary for
the service to function. We don't use cookies for analytics or
advertising. The marketing site (**feedbot.dev**) doesn't set any
tracking cookies either.

| Cookie | Set when | Purpose | Duration | Third-party? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `fb_session` | After magic-link sign-in | Authenticates your dashboard requests | Up to 30 days; cleared on sign-out | No |
| `mlnonce` | When you request a magic link | Binds the magic link to the browser that asked for it (PKCE) | ~15 minutes | No |

Both cookies are `Secure`, `HttpOnly`, `SameSite=Strict` — they're not
readable by JavaScript, not sent on cross-site requests, and require
HTTPS.

## Why no banner?

Strictly-necessary cookies don't need consent under GDPR / ePrivacy
because the service can't function without them. If a future feature
introduces optional cookies, we'll add a banner with proper consent
controls before that feature ships.

## Your control

You can clear cookies for `app.feedbot.dev` at any time from your
browser's settings. That signs you out and you'll need to request a new
magic link.
