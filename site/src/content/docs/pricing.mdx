## FAQ

### Will self-host stay free?

Yes — forever, MIT. We don't believe in feature-stripping the OSS to push people to a paid tier. The cloud is **ops-as-a-service**, not a different product.

### What's the cloud going to cost?

We're calibrating. Likely a per-seat tier for small teams and a per-feedback tier for high-volume installs. Waitlist members will see prices ahead of public launch and get a launch discount.

### Can I migrate self-host → cloud (or back)?

Yes. Postgres dump → restore. Same schema both sides. We'll publish a migration guide when cloud goes live.

### What about LLM costs?

Same model both sides: you bring your own OpenAI / Anthropic key, costs are tracked in `llm_calls`, monthly budget caps prevent surprises. The cloud may later offer a managed LLM with usage billed inline, but BYO-key is the default.

### Do you offer support contracts for self-host?

Not at launch. We're focused on shipping the product. Once cloud is live we'll evaluate paid OSS support contracts for teams that prefer to run their own infra.
